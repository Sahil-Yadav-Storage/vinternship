## Amazing Experience with IIT Ropar

Sahil Yadav  
Student at Delhi Technological University (Formerly DCE)  

February 6, 2026

Since joining the Euclideans batch on January 14th, my internship under Prof. Sudarshan Iyengar at IIT Ropar has been a true masterclass in scaling up. Transitioning from personal hobby projects to enterprise-grade MERN applications felt daunting at first, but the structured and well-designed environment made the journey seamless.

### The Highlights

The ViBe platform content pushed me to stop “task-finishing” and start “deep-learning.” It emphasized understanding concepts deeply rather than just completing assignments.

Live sessions with Prof. Sudarshan Iyengar and the VLED Labs team were game-changers. Beyond technical insights, listening to peer success stories and engaging in the Monday/Wednesday/Friday quizzes kept the momentum strong and motivating.

Learning System Design from Nandan Prabhudesai completely changed how I approach and visualize large-scale software systems.
 
While we encountered some early technical issues on the platform, the VLED team’s rapid response—resolving everything within a week—was truly impressive and reassuring.

More than just writing code, the breakout rooms and collaborative sessions significantly sharpened my communication and teamwork skills.  

A huge thank you to NPTEL, IIT Ropar, and Delhi Technological University (DTU) for providing such a holistic and growth-oriented learning opportunity.
